Bridget Riley

2024 - Video editing, imitation, reinterpretation
A LOOK AT A CONTEMPORARY WORK

This is a short film about Bridget Riley’s “Rose Rose” poster for the London 2012 Summer Olympics, Riley remaining celebrated for her optically vibrant paintings. I chose to rely on the presence and importance of the horizontal line in the original poster to instill the dynamics and rhythm of sport practice, especially at a professional level.
By manipulating relationships between sports, colors and shapes through video editing, I tried to metaphorically highlight sport fields such as athletic tracks or swimming lanes : I wanted to replicate the feeling of movement, capturing the energy flowing through sport and the Olympic Games in general, which Bridget Riley did herself.
